FAQs

How do I start?

Press the red Enter button or the Sign Up button in the upper right-hand corner of the site. Once your account is created, it will be easy to log in each time you come to the site.

How do I save my answers?

Your answers are automatically saved every time you click the button for Next or Previous Question or press the X on the top right corner of your screen. Please be sure not to leave the site without taking one of those actions to make sure that your work is saved.

Are my answers kept private?

Yes, your answers are totally confidential. It is up to you to decide if anyone can see your answers and, if so, whom. Our site is secured to the highest standards with SSL and HTTPS.

Do I have to answer every question?

No, you can skip any question. Just press Next on the screen to move to the next question. Remember, you can always return at any time to a question you have skipped.

Can I change or edit a question once I’ve moved onto the next question?

Yes, you can go back to any question at any time and add something or make any changes you like.

Can I add photos to my LifeStory Challenge?

Yes. At the end of each chapter there will be a photo page. Here you will be given the opportunity to upload two pictures. You can always go back and change those images. You can also click on the button on the right, My Photo Album, to see all the pictures you have uploaded so far. You can also add extra pictures with captions in the Photo Gallery, which is at the bottom of the photo album. All these pictures will be included in your LifeStory Challenge book.

Can I send one of my answers to a friend or family member?

Yes. You simply click on Share My Story button on the left side of the Table of Contents page. You will be provided with a link that you can easily share via email with your loved ones.

How do I continue working on my LifeStory Challenge when I come back to the site?

After you log back in, the system will ask you if want to continue working where you left off the last time. If you do want to continue there, just click Yes. If you want to start on a new chapter or a new question, just click No and the system will take you back to the Table of Contents where you can choose what to work on next.

When I finish my LifeStory Challenge, what do I do?

When you are done answering all the questions, you simply click on the Preview or Publish My LifeStory button on the Table of Contents page. You will have three options: (1) You can download a PDF of your manuscript. This will include all your answers with pre-written headings in place of the questions. Please note that you cannot download a PDF onto your mobile device. (2) You can navigate to your own personal website where you will have a beautiful digital version of your LifeStory. This site is accessible only to you and whomever you invite to see it. Your loved ones can leave you feedback there on your stories. (3) You can order a beautiful, customized book of your LifeStory. Just contact us directly at info@lifejourneysmedia.com

Is there a time limit on finishing my LifeStory Challenge?

There is no time limit but we encourage you to answer three to six questions (a half-chapter to a whole chapter) each week to keep your momentum high. At that pace, the process should take between 6 and 12 weeks.

I think I lost my work. What do I do?

Your answers are automatically saved every time you click the button for Next or Previous Question or press the X on the top right corner of your screen. If there was a power outage and you were not able to take any action, it is possible some of your work was lost. Feel free to call us at 617-429-6372 and talk with our staff or email us at info@lifejourneysmedia.com. We will get back to you as quickly as we can.

I can't remember my password. What should I do?

We can help! Feel free to call us at 617-429-6372 and talk with our staff or email us at info@lifejourneysmedia.com. We will get back to you as quickly as we can.

What if I’ve finished the LifeStory Challenge and want to continue writing my Life Story?

That’s great! Stay tuned for the additional LifeStory products coming out shortly. To hear about our new products, send us an email at info@lifejourneysmedia.com so we can add you to our Announcement email list.
